# myLifeTracker

A mobile life tracking app that visualises your daily progress through a growing tree model. Log your health, habits, and routine and watch your tree grow.
Letting users to recieve visualised data after each entry results in the reward of a visualised tree evoloving according to their progress, The tree has several stages it evolves into "Seed", "Seedling", "Sprout", "Sapling" all these stage represent the progress and growth you have made from when you last logged an entry.The immidiate rewrd effect will motivate users to continue  staying on top of their health, habits and daily routines.


Key Features

Today — dashboard with pulse score, tree, and notices



Physical — log sleep, water, and movement daily

Routine — manage tasks, meetings, and deadlines

Habits — build daily habits with streaks and XP



Tech Stack
-React Native + Expo go

-AsyncStorage (offline, on-device data)

-React Native SVG 

-Three.js

Intructions 
1.Open app and you'll see today's screen- daily dashboard with tree model and pulse score.

2.log your heal metric on the red FAB button and log in your sleep, diet and gym report for the day.

2.Manage your day by Tapping the FAB white button and tick them off as you go, add task, meetings and dealines.

3.Watch your tree grow and every time you hit a goal and earn more XP, the more XP you earn the bigger your tree model gets.

4.Pull down  to refresh and reset your tasks.
